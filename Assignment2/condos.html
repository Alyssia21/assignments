The condos at Destin Trails ⊛

What is a beach condo?
Our luxury condos are permanent structures four feet off the ground.Each condo has glass and marble walls, hardwood floors, and a skylight that can be opened.
How are the beach condos furnished?
Each condo is furnished with a queen-sized bed, large-screen Tv, and a mini-fridge. Your luxury experience includes A private bathroom with waterfall shower and whirlpool tub for two.
What should I bring?
Bathing suits, beach attire, and sun screen.
